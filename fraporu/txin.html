Reykunyu

1. txopu noun (txo-pu)
fear
Conjugation
singular dual trial plural (> 3)
subjective txopu me-topu pxe-topu (ay-)topu
agentive txopu-l me-topu-l pxe-topu-l (ay-)topu-l
patientive txopu-t(i) me-topu-t(i) pxe-topu-t(i) (ay-)topu-t(i)
dative txopu-r(u) me-topu-r(u) pxe-topu-r(u) (ay-)topu-r(u)
genitive txopu-ä me-topu-ä pxe-topu-ä (ay-)topu-ä
topical txopu-ri me-topu-ri pxe-topu-ri (ay-)topu-ri
Usage examples
Avatar (1):
(Tsu'tey) Tsamsiyu, txopu rä’ä si, tsamsiyu!
Warrior, don't be afraid, warrior!
Na'viteri (2):
'Uol ikranit txopu sleykolatsu, taluna po tsìk yawo.
Something must have frightened the banshee, because it suddenly took to the air.
Ngeyä aylì'ul akxapnga' txopu ke sleyku oet kaw'it.
Your threatening words don't scare me one bit.
